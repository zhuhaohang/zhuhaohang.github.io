Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Ocean Science (AI4Ocean). I am actively exploring Graph Neural Networks (GNN) to model dynamic ocean systems, focusing on spatio-temporal data mining and physics-informed deep learning.

Geometric Modeling & Decision Support for Road Damage Assessment
CV · 3D Reconstruction · Decision Tree
Designed a monocular vision system to estimate pothole areas by integrating ZoeDepth for metric depth estimation and Pinhole Camera Models. Further developed a decision support system for autonomous driving maintenance strategies based on the geometric analysis.
Outcomes: 1 Paper Submitted to IEEE T-ITS (arXiv:2505.21049) & 1 Paper Accepted by IEEE ICSIDP 2024 (EI)
Robust Small Target Detection via Multi-Sensor Fusion
Multimodal Feature Fusion · YOLO · Radar Signal Processing
Developed a hierarchical fusion architecture (Data, Feature, Decision levels) combining Infrared and Microwave Radar data. Improved YOLO algorithm and optimized CFAR algorithm to achieve high-precision detection of small targets under complex interference.
