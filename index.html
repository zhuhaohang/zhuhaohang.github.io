Geometric Modeling and Decision Support for Road Damage Assessment

Designed a monocular vision system to estimate pothole areas by integrating ZoeDepth for metric depth estimation with pinhole camera models. Further developed a decision support module for autonomous-driving maintenance strategies based on geometric analysis.

Outcomes: 1 paper submitted to IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems (arXiv:2505.21049) and 1 paper accepted by IEEE ICSIDP 2024 (EI).
